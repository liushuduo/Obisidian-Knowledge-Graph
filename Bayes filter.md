根据贝叶斯定理可以得到：
$$
p(x_t|z_{1:t},u_{1:t}) = \frac{p(z_t|x_t, z_{1:t-1}, u_{1:t})p(x_t|z_{1:t-1}, u_{1:t})}{p(z_t|z_{1:t-1}, u_{1:t})}\tag{1}\label{eq:1}
$$
进一步假设随机过程满足马尔可夫性（[[Markov property]]），则有
$$
p(z_t|x_t, z_{1:t - 1}, u_{1:t})=p(z_t|x_t)
$$
